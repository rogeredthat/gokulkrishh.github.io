Frequently asked questions

How much time and money will it take to launch my idea?

We charge for monthly releases and the development time depends on the technical requirement for your idea. Usually, we aim to build a viable launch product in a month.

How many developers & designers will I get to work on my idea?

Your team will consist of a backend and a front end developer supervised by a technical product manager keeping things on track.

My project is half developed and unfinished, can you help me out?

If the codebase is not in a good shape then we will prefer to re-write it. Sometimes, that’s cheaper and quicker. Let’s talk.

Do you work with existing startups?

Yes, we do. We offer tailor made teams using specific technologies for existing startups. Get in touch with us, so we can understand your tech stack and how we can help you scale affordably and efficiently.

I have a rough idea that I have been mulling over for some time. What should I do?

Signup with us and we will brainstorm together. All you need is an idea and commitment to iterate and succeed.

What’s your track record?

As a company, we’ve been building web products since Jan 2013 in ideas as diverse as food, internet security, dedicated marketplaces and photo sharing. As a team, we’ve been building products since 1999 with a successful track record in web and mobile technologies.

I don’t have enough funds to invest in the beginning, do you consider taking equity?

We do not take equity. It’s simpler for all concerned in the long run.

Is my idea safe in your hands?

100%. We’re incorporated out of Singapore for it’s great track record for transparency. We use NDAs and a secure platform to communicate on ideas, specifications, code and progress.

What will happen after launching my idea?

We will continue to support you on either a development or maintenance basis.

What makes you special?

Our startup value system understands the value of time and money to an entrepreneur and emphasizes precise feature development, quick iteration and accuracy of time and budget.

What technologies do you use?

We use cutting edge technologies like Python, Django/Flask/Tornado, Mysql/Mongodb, Scala, Lift, Clojure, Javascript, Backbone js.

Are you nice guys?

Well, guys and gals. And yes, we are! Are you? :)
